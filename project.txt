PopDown Menu Animation Calculation:

1.to get the position of the div, I found out where it started (at 0 px).
2.i used negitive margins to position the div up ward above the page.
	i calculated the position of the margin by using the line as a guide and estimated where to stop, i roughly guessed -100px at first, realising i couldnt see the menu, then i went down by ten(-90px)up by 5 (-95px)and up to (-97px)and up by 1 (-98px). 
	When i went down by ten i could see the menu but too much of it, i kept going up in my guessing until i could see the line. to test i went to -97px, where i could see the line and when i went to -98px i couldnt see the line.

	
	
Gallry Image Menu Height:

	2.At first i positioned the panel by using the same margin as the image margin, then to get the panel inline with the image itself, i increased my margin to 27%. At first i tried 25, then 30, then 27 percent.
		This way it looks as if the div can be positioned over the images.
